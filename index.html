1. How to get an admission in a Chinese university?
Once you contact us and study your profile we suggest you many Chinese universities that can accept you with different options.
2. Do you offer full scholarship?
We have different offers, it depends on your grades. Some universities offer partial and some offer full scholarships.
3. Which country is safe and better for higher study?
China can be a safe and viable option for higher education, with many highly-ranked universities and well-regarded programs. However, students should be aware of potential cultural and language barriers and do their research to ensure they enroll in a reputable institution. Ultimately, the decision to pursue higher education in China will depend on each student's individual circumstances and preferences.

Yes, it's possible to get a scholarship with your low cGPA.
